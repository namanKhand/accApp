  > 
  > Does self-discipline fail you? do2gether transforms goal setting from a solitary struggle into a shared commitment. Link up with an accountability partner who acts as your co-pilot, not just a spectator.
  > 
  > **Core Features:**
  > - **Structured Goal Setting:** Define your habit, frequency, and deadline with ease.
  > - **Photo Proof Check-ins:** Proof beats promises. Upload a daily photo to verify your progress and keep your streak alive.
  > - **Social Nudges:** If you miss a check-in, your partner gets notified and can send you a "Nudge" to get you back on track.
  > - **Streak Visualization:** Track your consistency with a beautiful streak calendar and progress bar.
  > 
  > Whether you're hitting the gym, reading more, or learning a new skill, do2gether ensures you never have to stay consistent alone.
