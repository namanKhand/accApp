## Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: February 22, 2026**

**do2gether** ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you.

### 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information**: Email, display name, and phone number when you register.
- **User Content**: Photos uploaded for habit check-ins and goal descriptions.
- **Usage Data**: Technical information such as device type and app interactions.

### 2. How We Use Information
- To provide and maintain our habit-tracking service.
- To facilitate social accountability features (sharing progress with your chosen partners).
- To send you "Nudges" and goal reminders.

### 3. Sharing of Information
- Your check-in photos and goal progress are shared ONLY with the accountability partners you explicitly invite.
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## Terms of Service
By using **do2gether**, you agree to the following:

### 1. Content Standards
You are solely responsible for the content (photos/text) you upload. You may not upload offensive, illegal, or abusive content. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates these standards.

### 2. Reporting & Blocking
To comply with Apple/Google safety guidelines:
- Users can report offensive content or abusive behavior.
- Users can block accountability partners at any time.
- Any reported content will be reviewed within 24 hours and removed if found in violation.

### 3. Termination
We reserve the right to terminate your account if you violate these terms.
