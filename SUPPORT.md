## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I invite a partner?**
A: Go to your Goal details and tap "Invite Partner".

**Q: I forgot to check in yesterday, can I fix my streak?**
A: Currently, streaks are strictly enforced to keep you accountable! If you miss a day, your streak will reset.

**Q: How to delete my account?**
A: Go to the Profile screen and tap on "Delete Account" to permanently remove your data.
